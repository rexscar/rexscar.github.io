About

Who am I?

I am an Electrical and Electronic Engineering student. My interests are in robotics, embedded systems and control engineering. It was my dream growing up to be able to build the machines of fiction I saw in the movies I watched, I loved the thought of being able to build whatever system or machine as long as I knew enough. This led me to learn as much as I could.

Along with studying as an electrical and electronic engineer, I built my skills as a developer (C++,HTML,CSS ,MATLAB ,Python, Arduino ,PLC ladder programing). I also studied several design software such as AutoCAD and kicad, and whatever I needed to know to build the projects I wanted ,from obstacle avoiding robots to home automation systems. My wish and hope is to be at the frontline as technology evolves around the world and in Africa.

Embedded Systems

I have studied and worked with several microcontrollers and sensors in building different problem solving embedded applications . I have frequently worked with the atmega328p, raspberry pi, esp32 and PIC18 microcontrollers.

Programming

I am skilled in both high level languages and low level machine language . I am good with C++,C,python,HTML, CSS,SQL,MATLAB and VHDL.

3D Modeling and CAD

I often model projects to help visualise my idea. I work with AutoCAD invertor, Fusion and AutoCad electrical (for electrical services design)

Circuit Design

As an electrical and electronics engineer Circuitry design is key in building system . I am able to build out and stimulate circuitries using multisim, kicad and proteus.
